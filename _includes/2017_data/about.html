About

LearnTo iOS is an 8 week training course hosted by Charged, Secure Broadcast & NSBelfast that will teach the fundamentals to iOS app development, starting Tuesday, May 15th. The aim of the course is to go from “0 to app” in 4 months.

This free Bootcamp is for students, postgrads or anyone with relevant experience who wishes to get into iOS development.

The format will be as follows, 8 weeks of 2 hour sessions aimed at grasping the concepts of iOS Development with mentor support. With the core fundamentals taught, participants will then start their app development with 4 weeks of drop in sessions for support. Finally a further 8 weeks of independent app development with online mentoring available.

At the end of the twelve week app development period, participants will pitch their app to the judges with prizes totalling up to £3k up for grabs.
